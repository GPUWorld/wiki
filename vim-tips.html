Split Window

Mode Switch

  • change two vertically split windows to horizonally split 
    Ctrl-W t Ctrl-W Shift-K
  • change two horizontally split windows to vertically split: 
    Ctrl-W t Ctrl-W Shift-H
  • Explanations:
    • Ctrl-W t makes the topleft window current
    • Ctrl-W Shift-K moves the current window to full-width at the very top
    • Ctrl-W Shift-H moves the current window to full-height at far left
  • References: 
    :help opening-window
