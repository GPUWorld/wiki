Manipulating Processes Symbolically with procps

  • Manipulating Processes Symbolically with procps
    • skill
    • snice

skill

send signals to processes by name, terminal, username, or PID.

e.g. 

$ skill -STOP pts/2
# release from the grip of sleeping death
$ skill -CONT pts/2

snice

renice processes. 

$ snice +5 luser
